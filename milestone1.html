Portable Refrigeration

Our first milestone: build and deploy low-cost, portable refrigeration units for market traders and smallholders.

Progress

Overall milestone progress: 1/5

About this milestone

Our portable refrigeration prototype is designed with low energy consumption and compatibility with intermittent solar power, minimizing reliance on batteries while maintaining safe temperatures for common produce.

Sub-steps

  1. Prototype build

    Design, assemble and test the first functioning unit (mechanical design, insulation, cooling components, basic controls).

  2. Trader trials

    Deploy units with market traders for real-world testing — collect feedback on usability, capacity and costs.

  3. Farmer rollout

    Expand to farmer networks and aggregators to reduce transport losses and increase shelf-life.

  4. IoT logging

    Implement basic data logging (temperature, runtime, energy draw) to guide future improvements and traceability.

  5. Thermal supply chain

    Secure sources for thermal battery / phase-change materials and local manufacturing partners.
